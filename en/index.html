What is he accused of?

Dmitry Klepikov is accused of "unlawful communication".

Why is he innocent?

As Dmitry Bogatov, Dmitry Klepikov ran a Tor exit relay and anyone could post any information from his IP address.

Running an exit relay is not illegal in Russia, and he's not charged with that. Tor is a special software which routes Internet traffic over random circuits builded from servers located around the world. Exit relays are used as last servers in circuit, that's why their adresses appears in logs. Dmitry's relay was online last time at 11 February 2018. In support of Dmitry Bogatov, he called his relay name KActionLibre, and now he is accused with similar charges.
